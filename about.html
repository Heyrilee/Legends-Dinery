About Us

We believe a happy adventure starts with a happy tummy

Whether you are about to start your next quest or you are exhausted and hungry after a long day of fighting Hilichurls, our mission is to satisfy your hunger while bringing up your defense and stamina bars up to maximum potential. All our ingredients have been sourced all around the whole region of Teyvat by our team of professional adventurors. Taking inspiration from Monstadt all the way to Inazuma our recipes are like a delightful fusion of all the elements. Our mission is to provide our customers with the best culinary experience.

Never go adventuring on an empty stomach! Enjoy a delicious meal from Legends Dinery!
